Claudia's Cookies

Claudia’s Cookies are a customer favorite at Blackstone Grill. Each cookie is baked fresh using classic recipes and quality ingredients.

We offer a rotating selection of cookie flavors, including chocolate chip, oatmeal raisin, and seasonal specials. Cookies can be enjoyed on their own or added to any meal.

Stop by the Blackstone Grill food truck to try Claudia’s Cookies, or ask about including them with your catering order.
